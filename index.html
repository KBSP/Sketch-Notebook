Using Sketch Notebook Plugin

  • First steps

    After it’s installed, start commenting your documents by going to “Plugins > Sketch Notebook” at the menu bar of Sketch

  • Add comment

    Select any layer and run “Add comment”. A new comment will be added to the sidebar

    Keyboard Shortcut

  • Reorder

    Reorder and hit update. Comments will be automatically realigned and renumbered

    Keyboard Shortcut

  • Edit and realign

    Change your comments and update. Everything will be in its place after that

    Keyboard Shortcut

  • Update your design

    Keep designing and hit update. Comments indicators will be relocated to its new positions

    Keyboard Shortcut

  • Delete comments

    Remove the layer group that contains the unwanted comment(s) and hit update. Again, they will automatically reorder and renumber

    Keyboard Shortcut

  • Toggle Notes Visibility

    Choose whether you like to show or hide your comments by toggling its visibility

    Keyboard Shortcut
